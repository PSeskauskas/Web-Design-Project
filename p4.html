Our Plans For The Future

Here at Seskauskas's Fast Foods, we thrive on giving excellent service, and we'd like that service to be available to the public all over Dublin. Therefore, we would like to announce our future plans to open restaurants in Dundrum Shopping Centre, Liffey Valley Shopping Centre and The Square Shopping Centre. With the expansion of the number of stores, we plan on expanding our menu selection to include new and exclusive dishes that can only be found in our restaurants. We have also looked into obtaining a liquor license and building bars in our current store and our future planned stores.

Alongside the plans for our new restaurants, we plan on creating a delivery service to serve the surrounding areas of our restaurants. This service would be implemented online on this website or the customer could call into one of our restaurants and order anything from our menu, over the phone. All of the menu deals that are currently available and that will be available, will apply through our future delivery system to cater the customer's needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?
Yes, we do take reservations, if you would like to reserve a table for a group of up to 12 people please click here
Are you open on Bank/Public holidays?
Yes, we are open every day of the year except for Christmas Day. Our working hours are available on our Contact Us page
Do you have vegetarian, vegan, gluten free options?
Yes, we have some vegetarian/vegan options on our menu such as our soups. Some of our dishes are gluten free, if you would like to see gluten free options in our restaurant, you could ask one of our waiters/waitresses and they would happily explain our gluten free options.
Do you accept gift cards?
Yes, we do accept some gift cards such as One4All gift cards and Perx gift cards.
Is there parking nearby?
Yes, there is a Q-Park facility in Stephens' Green which is located approximately 5 minutes away from our restaurant by foot.
Where can I get nutritional info about your menu?
You can ask our waiters/waitresses for nutritional information on any item available on our menu.
Do you serve alcohol?
No, we haven't obtained our liquor license as of yet, but in the future we plan on serving alcohol
